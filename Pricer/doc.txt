Pour le modele Black-Scholes (s = vol BS)
S=spot

on dit dS/S = r(t).dt + s.dW

si s=0 (cas deterministe)
d (ln(S)) = r(t).dt
ln(S) = integral(r(t).dt)
S = S0.exp(integral(r(t).dt))

si s<>0 et r constant
S = S0.exp(r.t + s.Wt - 0.5*s*t^2))

dS_stochastic = S.s.dW
